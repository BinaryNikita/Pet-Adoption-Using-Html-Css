6 Steps to adopt a pet

1

Research: Understand pet ownership responsibilities and breed traits.

2

Search: Know which pet is your best fit and start your journey to finding a perfect companion.

3

Visit: Schedule a visit to meet available pets at the shelter or rescue.

4

Apply: Complete the adoption application process with necessary details.

5

Prepare: Get your home ready with pet essentials like food, bedding, and toys.

6

Adopt: Bring your new pet home and start creating routines for care and bonding.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I adopt a pet?
To adopt a pet, visit our adoption page, fill out an application, and schedule a meet-and-greet!
2. What is the adoption fee?
Our adoption fees vary based on the pet and include vaccinations, spaying/neutering, and microchipping.
3. Do you have any special adoption events?
Yes! We host regular adoption events. Check our events page for the latest updates.
4. Can I return a pet if it doesn’t work out?
Yes, we have a return policy to ensure the well-being of both pets and adopters.
5. What supplies do I need for my new pet?
You will need food, water bowls, a bed, toys, and grooming supplies. We provide a checklist upon adoption.
